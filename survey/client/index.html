Welcome!

Pozdravljeni!

Volumetric data is information about the inside of something in 3D - it's like being able to see and measure what's inside an object, for instance, a loaf of bread, a cloud, or a human body, and not just the outside. This kind of data helps doctors, meteorologists, physicists, and geologists to see and understand things in three dimensions rather than just flat pictures. There are many different techniques for visualizing such data, which all affect your spatial perception in different ways.

Volumetrični podatki so podatki, ki opisujejo notranjost nečesa tridimenzionalnega - naj bo to hlebec kruha, oblak ali človeško telo. Tovrstni podatki pomagajo zdravnikom, meteorologom, fizikom in geologom razumeti naravne pojave precej bolje kot le 2D slike. Obstaja mnogo različnih tehnik za vizualizacijo volumetričnih podatkov, ki na različne načine vplivajo na vašo prostorsko predstavo.

As part of our study, we are interested in how different volumetric rendering techniques affect the preceived distance between structures in volumetric data. The results of this study will help us design even better visualization techniques.

S to uporabniško študijo želimo ugotoviti, kako različne vizualizacijske tehnike vplivajo na zaznavanje razdalj med strukturami v volumetričnih podatkih. Rezultati študije nam bodo pomagali načrtovati še boljše vizualizacijske tehnike.
